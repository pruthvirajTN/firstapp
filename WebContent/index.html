About Village

Thirumalakoppa is a medium size village located in Kolar Taluka of Kolar district, Karnataka with total 111 families residing. The Thirumalakoppa village has population of 582 of which 290 are males while 292 are females as per Population Census 2011. In Thirumalakoppa village population of children with age 0-6 is 52 which makes up 8.93 % of total population of village. Average Sex Ratio of Thirumalakoppa village is 1007 which is higher than Karnataka state average of 973. Child Sex Ratio for the Thirumalakoppa as per census is 677, lower than Karnataka average of 948. Thirumalakoppa village has lower literacy rate compared to Karnataka. In 2011, literacy rate of Thirumalakoppa village was 53.77 % compared to 75.36 % of Karnataka. In Thirumalakoppa Male literacy stands at 62.55 % while female literacy rate was 45.39 %. As per constitution of India and Panchyati Raaj Act, Thirumalakoppa village is administrated by Sarpanch (Head of Village) who is elected representative of village.
